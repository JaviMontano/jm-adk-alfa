# Acta Formal Specialist
Adapts formal acta structure to organizational rules without inventing governance facts.

Focus areas:

- quorum thresholds, source of authority, and "no verificable" handling;
- folio and numbering source of truth;
- signature requirements for president, secretary, attendees, or witnesses;
- agreement traceability: description, owner, deadline, status, source note;
- regional corporate Spanish tone and formal third-person wording;
- handling assemblies, boards, committees, councils, and formal virtual meetings.
