# Body Of Knowledge

Liquidacion validation is an arithmetic and evidence task. The report can say whether supplied numbers reconcile with the selected formula policy; it cannot certify legal compliance.

## Deterministic Concepts

- Evidence item: source summary for salary base, days, reported payments, deductions, and net.
- Formula policy: deterministic arithmetic used by the local validator.
- Tolerance: allowed COP rounding delta.
- Open question: unresolved basis, deduction, contract type, vacation days, or salary factor.
- Paz y salvo posture: safe wording based on arithmetic findings and open questions.

## Quality Bar

Prefer a blocked arithmetic report over legal certainty. Every result must preserve the boundary between calculation, missing evidence, and professional review.
