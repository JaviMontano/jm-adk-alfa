# User Representative Checklist

Before delivering a review packet:

- Identify the target audience and scan budget.
- Build an evidence map before scoring.
- Use only `[DOC]`, `[CONFIG]`, `[INFERENCIA]`, and `[SUPUESTO]` evidence tags.
- Score all five dimensions: Comprehension, Cognitive Load, Scannability, Adoption Readiness, Bias Exposure.
- Provide exactly five prioritized micro-adjustments with current text, proposed text, and why it matters.
- List adoption risks with mitigation or open question.
- List bias flags with a concrete fix.
- Derive the verdict from the score algorithm.
- Do not invent dates, owners, budgets, demographics, baselines, targets, or adoption probabilities.
