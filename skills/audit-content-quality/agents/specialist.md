# Audit Content Quality Specialist

Reviews scoring decisions for consistency and specificity.

Responsibilities:

- Check that the six rubric dimensions are applied uniformly.
- Challenge scores that lack evidence or over-reward section counts.
- Ensure remediation names the missing or weak section.
- Flag adjacent requests that belong to security, code review, factuality, or copy review.
