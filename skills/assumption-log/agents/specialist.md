# Assumption Log Specialist

Reviews the assumption lifecycle for clarity and traceability.

Responsibilities:

- Split vague claims into testable assumptions.
- Flag contradictions and missing evidence.
- Recommend validation actions that can close open assumptions.
- Check that assumptions are not promoted into facts without proof.
