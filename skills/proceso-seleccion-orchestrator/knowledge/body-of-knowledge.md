# Body Of Knowledge

Selection-process tracking is a provenance problem. The board is trustworthy only when every contact, stage, risk, and next action links to a supplied evidence item.

## Deterministic Concepts

- Evidence item: a bounded note, email excerpt, interview summary, or manual entry with an ID, source type, ISO received date, and short summary.
- Contact: a person or group tied to a role and an evidence reference. Do not invent titles from context.
- Stage: a process step with a stable sequence, owner, status, reason, and evidence reference.
- Next action: exactly one action tied to one stage and one evidence item.
- Blocker: any condition that prevents a reliable board, including missing evidence, ambiguous dates, conflicting status, or promised outcomes.

## Quality Bar

A good board is compact, auditable, and reproducible. It favors `blocked` over false certainty, preserves unknowns as `[OPEN]`, and never derives facts from current time, email access, web search, or memory.
