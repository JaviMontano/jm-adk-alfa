# Deliverable Checklist

Use this asset as the stable checklist for a `funnel-analytics` deliverable.

## Required Checks

- Funnel objective, audience, unit, source owner, time window, and timezone are explicit.
- Every funnel step has event/source, numerator, denominator, exclusions, owner, and evidence status.
- Conversion and drop-off formulas define unit, window, deduplication, and exclusions before interpretation.
- Missing instrumentation, sample limits, bot/internal filters, identity stitching, and freshness risks are marked `not verified`.
- Segment analysis includes sample-size, cohort, and comparability cautions.
- Recommendations are separated into instrumentation fixes, research tasks, product changes, and experiments.
- Experiments include target metric, guardrail metric, required instrumentation, and validation method.
- Privacy-sensitive identifiers are minimized, redacted, or aggregated.
- The final deliverable references validation limits and next evidence needed.
