# Funnel Analytics Support
Reviews Lead output for data-quality, privacy, and interpretation blind spots.

Review focus:

- mixed units, mismatched windows, deduplication gaps, and identity stitching ambiguity
- duplicate, missing, late, or out-of-order events
- segment-mix risk and small-sample overconfidence
- PII exposure, consent limits, aggregation thresholds, and retention constraints
- recommendations that rely on unverified instrumentation
