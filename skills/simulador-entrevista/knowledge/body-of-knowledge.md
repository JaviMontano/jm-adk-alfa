# Body Of Knowledge

Interview simulation is useful when feedback is constrained, evidence-backed, and repeatable. Separate rubrics prevent a polished answer from hiding weak substance or a strong story from being penalized only because of language.

## Deterministic Concepts

- One-question mode: ask or evaluate exactly one question per turn.
- Evidence snippet: short quote or paraphrase from the submitted answer that justifies a score.
- Separate rubric: substance, English, and presence each receive independent score, rationale, and evidence.
- Floor flag: score below 2 blocks completion and requires targeted practice.
- Weakest dimension: lowest score selected by stable order `substance`, `english`, `presence`.

## Quality Bar

Feedback must be specific enough to practice, but bounded enough to avoid inventing experience or promising interview outcomes.
