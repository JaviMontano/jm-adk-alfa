# Body of Knowledge

## Core Model

The skill turns offer negotiation into a reproducible decision packet:

1. Normalize supplied offer facts.
2. Apply hard acceptance filters.
3. Compute PIVOTE from six bounded dimensions.
4. Rank only offers that pass every filter.
5. Draft counterproposal moves only from supplied evidence.

## PIVOTE Dimensions

- Purpose: strategic fit with the user's direction.
- Income: compensation fit against the supplied floor.
- Viability: operational feasibility and clarity of scope.
- Optionality: ability to preserve future paths and parallel streams.
- Traction: evidence of momentum, decision clarity, or sponsor support.
- Energy: sustainability of cadence and workload.

## Non-Invention Rule

Never invent salary benchmarks, competing offers, tax effects, equity value, immigration constraints, or recruiter promises. Mark them as open questions unless the user provides evidence.
