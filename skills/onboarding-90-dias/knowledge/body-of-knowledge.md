# Body of Knowledge

## 30/60/90 Model

- 30 days: learn, listen, map systems, and build trust through a small artifact.
- 60 days: contribute to a bounded deliverable and reduce ambiguity.
- 90 days: own one improvement, document learning, and propose a sustainable cadence.

## Sustainable Planning

Cap sustained weekly work at 45 hours. Keep each phase to four priorities or fewer. Require recovery boundaries, stakeholder validation, and observable deliverables instead of vague commitment language.

## Non-Invention Rule

Do not invent stakeholders, dates, role expectations, promotion outcomes, or hidden company goals. Mark missing evidence as an open question.
