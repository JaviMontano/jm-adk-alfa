## Local Interpretation

- [DOC] GA4 events are grouped into automatic, enhanced measurement, recommended, and custom events.
- [DOC] Recommended events should be preferred when the business action matches an official event.
- [DOC] Measurement Protocol supplements tagging and is not a replacement for gtag, GTM, or Firebase tagging.
- [DOC] Consent and privacy review is required because Google places responsibility for applicable consent and privacy compliance on the implementer.
- [DOC] Tag Assistant, GTM Preview, GA4 DebugView, and Realtime reports are the verification surfaces used by this skill.
