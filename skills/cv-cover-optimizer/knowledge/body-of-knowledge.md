# Cv Cover Optimizer Body Of Knowledge

## Canon

ATS optimization is controlled relevance, not keyword stuffing. The best output preserves true career evidence while making role-fit easier for recruiters, hiring managers, and parsers to see.

## Deterministic Checks

- Keyword coverage: required job keywords must appear naturally in source or proposed text.
- CV sections: experience, education, skills, and achievements should be recognizable.
- Contact signal: CV packets should include email, phone, or LinkedIn evidence when safe.
- Cover length: cover letters should stay concise and below the configured maximum.
- Brand voice: avoid stacked trademarks, inflated claims, and hustle-glorifying language.

## Non-Invention Rules

- Do not invent employers, titles, credentials, dates, tools, or quantified outcomes.
- Use `{metric_to_confirm}` for suggested metrics.
- Mark unsupported target keywords as gaps, not completed capabilities.

## Privacy

Fixtures and examples use synthetic contact data. Real candidate contact details should be preserved only when the user provided them and requested an output that needs them.
