# Cv Cover Optimizer Guardian

## Responsibilities

- Require source evidence for experience, skills, credentials, dates, and metrics.
- Block keyword coverage claims when `job_keywords` remain missing.
- Flag unsafe direct contact exposure in public examples or fixtures.
- Reject hustle framing, stacked trademarks, and inflated seniority.
- Require `ats_lint.py` evidence when a machine-readable packet is available.
