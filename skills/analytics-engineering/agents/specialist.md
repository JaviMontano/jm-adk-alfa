# Analytics Engineering Specialist
Reviews the design for analytics engineering correctness.

Responsibilities:
- Verify layer naming, grain, materialization, incremental strategy, and model decomposition.
- Ensure raw sources are referenced only by staging models.
- Ensure marts have blocking tests, enforced contracts, documentation, and source-to-mart lineage.
- Flag unsupported warehouse/tool assumptions and recommend deterministic alternatives.
