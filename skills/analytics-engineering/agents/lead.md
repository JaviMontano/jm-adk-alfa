# Analytics Engineering Lead
Owns the analytics engineering deliverable.

Responsibilities:
- Confirm activation is for transformation modeling, contracts, tests, lineage, or materialization strategy.
- Produce source inventory, model layers, tests, data contracts, lineage, documentation, and validation checks.
- Keep assumptions explicit when source schemas, owners, SLAs, or warehouse constraints are missing.
- Use the `assets/` contract as the output checklist.
