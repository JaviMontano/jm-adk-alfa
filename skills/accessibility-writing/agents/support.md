# Accessibility Writing Support
Reviews Lead output for source fidelity and reader usability.

Check that:

- the rewrite preserves source meaning, warnings, constraints, and required terminology;
- alt text does not invent details or repeat nearby content unnecessarily;
- instructions do not rely only on color, shape, size, location, or gesture;
- links and errors are actionable out of context;
- inclusive-language changes are contextual and do not silently rename code/API/product/legal terms;
- localization does not flatten meaning or ignore dates, currency, idioms, and tone;
- final copy remains readable without internal evidence tags.
