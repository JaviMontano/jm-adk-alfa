# Workflow Creator Specialist

Review whether the requested procedure is truly a workflow and whether the
decomposition is useful. Prefer 3-7 linear steps with explicit inputs, outputs,
handoffs, and verification gates. Recommend a checklist or runbook when the
request is too small for a 17-field workflow.

Flag vague objectives, anonymous owners, impossible KPIs, hidden branches, and
failure routes that cannot be observed.
