# Body Of Knowledge

Reference-network management is consent and provenance management. A contact can be useful only when the packet clearly states what they consented to, when, from which evidence, and which actions are allowed.

## Deterministic Concepts

- Evidence item: supplied note or message summary with ID, type, ISO date, source, and summary.
- Contact: privacy-safe reference label with relationship, consent status, evidence, last contact date, and allowed actions.
- Allowed action: action permitted by explicit consent and policy.
- Stale follow-up: last contact older than the configured threshold from packet `as_of`.
- Network edge: relationship between two contacts or candidate-contact relationship with evidence.

## Quality Bar

Prefer a blocked packet over unauthorized outreach. Do not infer consent from relationship strength, seniority, friendliness, or prior conversations.
